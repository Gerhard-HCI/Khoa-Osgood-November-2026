Dining Etiquette
🚫
No Fragrance — Critical
No perfume, cologne, scented hair products, or strongly fragranced detergents on any omakase dining day. This is the single most important etiquette point.
⏰
Punctuality
Omakase meals begin simultaneously for all guests. Arrive early. Many of Tokyo's finest restaurants are in quiet streets — allow generous transfer time.

Cash
Several restaurants at this programme level are cash only. Carry sufficient yen at all times. ATMs at 7-Eleven and Japan Post.

Counter Etiquette
Remove rings, bracelets, and watches at sushi counters — often made from precious single-piece hinoki cypress. Tipping not expected; say "Gochisousama deshita."
